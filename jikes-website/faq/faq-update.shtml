FAQ Update

You found something stated in these FAQs and you know it to be wrong? You would like to have it updated with something new, or remove an outdated topic from it?

Easy: just post to the Jikes-Dev mailing list with the "FAQDev" or "FAQUser" keyword somewhere in your subject, and the FAQ maintainers will take care of your request (the keyword makes it easy for us to search FAQ-related posts in the mailing list).
